How It Works

Simple steps to transform your relationship with music

1

Install Musoff

Download Musoff from the App Store and grant Screen Time permissions to enable app blocking.

2

Configure Blocked Apps

Select which music apps you want to block (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and more).

3

Experience the Shield

When you try to open a blocked app, Musoff's custom shield appears with a gentle reminder to listen to Quran instead.

4

Explore Quran Content

Access the comprehensive Quran library with multiple reciters, audio player, and beautifully organized surahs.

5

Track Your Progress

Monitor your streaks, days music-free, Quran listening time, and record meaningful moments in your private diary.
